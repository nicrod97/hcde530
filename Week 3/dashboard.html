Key Takeaways

What this dataset tells us about the collection process

  • No input validation on any field — free text allowed impossible ages, fictional names, and inconsistent capitalisation.
  • Significant duplicate inflation: ~30% of rows are near-duplicates of another row.
  • Tools and role labels need a controlled vocabulary or pick-list.
  • Country should use ISO codes, not free text.
  • Adding a CAPTCHA or bot-detection layer would remove the automated entries.
  • After deduplication and removal of clearly invalid rows, ~47 plausibly real responses remain out of 79 — a ~40% contamination rate.
